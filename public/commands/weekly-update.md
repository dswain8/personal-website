# Weekly Update — Generate

Generate your weekly status update for your manager.

## Step 1 — Gather this week's activity

Pull from two sources in parallel:

1. Read your task list / notes — look at what was completed, in progress, and blocked this week.
2. Search Slack for your activity this week:
   - `from:[your-handle] after:[THIS MONDAY'S DATE]`
   - Any key team channels you're active in

## Step 2 — Draft the update

Use **exactly** this format (plain text, no markdown, no bold):

```
Hey @[Manager Name], this is my weekly update for [Mon Day] - [Fri Day], [Year].

Updates

[Work Area]: [Concrete progress. What was done, found, shipped, decided.]

Blockers / Risks

[Work Area]: [What's blocked and what's needed to unblock.]

Next week - Focus

[Work Area]: [What will be driven or closed next week.]
```

## Rules
- One sentence per bullet — specific and factual, no fluff
- Plain text only — no markdown headers, no asterisks, no bullet symbols
- Omit entire sections (Updates / Blockers / Next week) if nothing to report
- Omit individual work areas with nothing to say
- Date range: Mon–Fri of the current week

## Step 3 — Present for approval

Show the draft. Do NOT send to Slack. Wait for explicit approval before posting.

After approval, save a copy to your notes or docs folder.
