# Daily Wrap — End-of-day summary

Summarize what got done today, what moved, and what needs tomorrow.

## Step 1 — Gather today's activity

Pull from:
1. Your task list / notes — what was completed, in progress, or abandoned today
2. Slack — your sent messages and threads you were active in today
3. Any docs, tickets, or specs you touched

## Step 2 — Write the wrap

Use this format:

```
DAILY WRAP — [Day, Date]

Done
• [Thing completed — be specific, one line each]

Moved
• [Thing that progressed but isn't done — where it stands]

Decisions made
• [Any calls made today, even small ones]

Tomorrow
• [Top 2-3 things that need to happen first]

Blockers
• [Anything stuck that needs action from someone else]
```

## Rules
- "Done" means actually finished, not just touched
- "Moved" is honest progress — don't pad it
- "Tomorrow" should be ruthlessly prioritised — max 3 items
- If nothing is blocked, omit that section
- Keep the whole thing under 15 lines — if it's longer, you're over-explaining

## Step 3 — Save

Save to your daily notes with today's date. Optionally share with your manager or team.
